[English](README_EN.md) | [中文](README.md)

## Where does this problem come from?

> When LLM applications move from prototype to production, deployment is no longer just "pushing code." It becomes a complex systems-engineering problem involving CI/CD for ML, cost optimization, elastic scaling, and security compliance. Traditional DevOps toolchains must evolve for AI scenarios.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you should be able to answer:
1. What are the fundamental differences between CI/CD for ML systems and traditional software?
2. How do you efficiently deploy and scale LLM inference services on Kubernetes?
3. What are the key levers for cost-optimizing production LLM applications?
