## Where does this problem come from?

> Between 2023 and 2024, large models moved from lab demos to real business scenarios: enterprise search, code assistants, and customer-service bots. But "the model runs" does not mean "the system is deliverable"—latency, cost, security, and maintainability became the keys to project success.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you should be able to answer:
1. What are the fundamental architectural differences between enterprise search and general search?
2. What are the core optimization goals for code assistants versus conversational systems?
3. What quality and safety assurances are needed for production-grade LLM applications?
